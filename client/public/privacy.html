Privacy Policy

SPMP Dashboards · Last updated: 20 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SPMP Dashboards (the “Service,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with providing advertising performance reporting to our clients. The Service is an internal reporting tool operated by Street Poller for the benefit of its clients and is not offered to the general public.

1. Who this applies to

The Service is used by authorised staff and by client accounts that we provision. Access is restricted by login credentials; there is no public sign-up. This policy applies to those authorised users and to the advertising data we process on behalf of our clients.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: the name and email address used to create a dashboard login, and a securely hashed password.
  • Advertising & analytics data: through official platform APIs (including the Meta Marketing API and the Google Ads API), we retrieve advertising performance metrics such as spend, impressions, clicks, reach, conversions, and campaign, ad set, and ad structure for the ad accounts our clients have authorised us to access.
  • Access tokens: API access tokens (for example, Meta System User tokens) that authorise the Service to read the above data. These are used solely to fetch reporting data.

We do not collect the personal profiles, messages, or contact lists of the end-users who saw or interacted with the ads. We work with aggregated advertising metrics only.

3. How we use the information

  • To authenticate users and provide access to their reporting dashboards.
  • To retrieve, aggregate, and display advertising performance reports.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Service.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their own marketing.

4. Platform data (Meta & Google)

Our use of information received from the Meta Marketing API complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies, and our use of the Google Ads API complies with the applicable Google API Services User Data Policy. Platform data is used only to provide the reporting features described above and is not transferred to any party except as necessary to operate the Service.

5. Data storage & security

Data is stored on managed cloud infrastructure. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes. Access tokens and reporting data are protected by access controls, and access is limited to authorised personnel. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for data in transit.

6. Data retention

We retain account and reporting data for as long as an account remains active and as needed to provide the Service. When access is revoked or an account is closed, associated tokens and data are deleted or de-identified within a reasonable period.

7. Data deletion & your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, and you may revoke the Service’s access to your advertising accounts at any time (for example, by removing the System User assignment or revoking the token in your Business settings). To make a request, contact us at the address below and we will action it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
